Plot Type

After the completion of the Run Analysis command, the results can be plotted in different ways. The possible plot styles are:
  • Raw data
  • Clean Data
  • Diameters by condition without baseline removed
  • Percentage change of diameters by condition
  • Millimeter change of diameters by condition
  • Mean Functional diameter by condition
  • Binned comparison of diameters by condition
  • Stimulus factor p-value
  • F-statistic between conditions
  • Functional t-test of difference between selected conditions
  • PCA factor loadings for selected conditions
  • Peak dilation comparison of conditions
